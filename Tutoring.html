UMSI Tutoring & Academic Support

The Academic Success Team at UMSI supports students inside and outside of the classroom.

Resources to Connect Students to Tutoring and Academic Support:

UMSI Peer Tutoring: Progamming Peer Tutoring

UMSI Programming Peer Tutors are availagle for SI and SIADS courses

UMSI Programming Peer Tutoring courses

  • SI 106 Programs, Information, and People
  • SI 206 Data-Oriented Programming
  • SI 506 Programming I
  • SI 507 Intermediate Programming
  • General Python support

UMSI MADS Progamming Peer Tutoring courses:

  • SIADS 505 Data Manipulation
  • General Python support

Email the UMSI Academic Success Team at umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu.

To book appointments with Programming Peer Tutors, visit the UMSI Tutoring Site.

UMSI Peer Tutoring: Math Peer Tutoring

UMSI Math Peer Tutors are available for SI courses, SIADS courses, and general math related content

UMSI Math Peer Tutoring subject areas:

  • Linear Algebra
  • Statistics
  • General Math related course support

UMSI MADS Math Peer Tutoring subject areas:

  • Linear Algebra
  • Statistics
  • General Math related course-support
  • SIADS 502 Math Methods I

Email the UMSI Academic Success Team at umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu.

To book appointments with Math Peer Tutors, visit the UMSI Tutoring Site.

Additional Academic Support Resources

Sweetland Writing Center

  • The Sweetland Center for Writing offers one-to-one tutoring for undergraduate and graduate students in faculty-led Writing Workshop and undergraduate Peer Writing Center. They also provides support for all multilingual and international undergraduate students.

English Language Institute

  • English Language Institute (ELI) provides English for Academic Purposes courses and resources, GSI preparation, and TESOL courses to prepare students to teach English as a second or foreign language. ELI is exclusively for those who are a part of the University of Michigan community.

Math Lab

  • The Math Lab provides free tutoring for mathematics courses mainly numbered through 217. Help is not regularly available for other courses

Services for Students with Disabilities

  • Services for Students with Disabilities (SSD) recognizes disability as an integral part of diversity. SSD is committed to creating an inclusive and equitable educational environment for disabled students.
